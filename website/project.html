Self-Analysis

Self-analysis tool that enables individuals to analyze their own mental health and gain insights into their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.

Parental Supervision

Parental supervision tool that provides parents with a way to monitor their child's mental health pattern to ensure their child's safety and well-being.

More

Collaborative Supervision

Collaborative supervision tool enables groups of individuals, such as colleagues or peers, to provide support and feedback to each other on their mental health and well-being. This can help to foster a sense of community and accountability, and enable individuals to work together to improve their mental health.

More

AR/VR Environment

AR/VR technology provides individuals with a virtual environment where they can practice and develop their coping skills and resilience in a safe and controlled setting. This technology provides a unique and immersive experience that can help individuals to manage stress and anxiety, and improve their mental health.
